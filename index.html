About

Adithya S.M is a B.Tech Graduate with a keen interest in Embedded Systems and Computer Vision. He had the opportunity to contribute to an Autonomous Rover project in collaboration with researchers from Frankfurt University, and his work was supported by the prestigious DAAD Scholarship, which allowed him to visit Germany and Poland. Adithya was part of the Frankfurt Robotics Science Team that participated in the European Rover Challenge 2022 finals in Kielce, Poland. Adithya has actively participated in numerous hackathons, showcasing his passion for innovation and problem-solving. As a strong researcher, he currently has a pending patent and a publication, reflecting his dedication to advancing knowledge and technology in his field.
